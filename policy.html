Privacy Policy

Min Wang (“we”, “us”, “our”) is an international software development company specialized in the mobile apps. This guideline will explain how Min Wang will collect, use and store your device information. We will use this Privacy Policy to explain the relevant details, including the following:

  • We will describe the types of device information we collect and their corresponding use in order for you to understand how we collect specific type of device information for a particular function, the reasons for its use and the methods of such collection.
  • Whilst using some features, we will collect your device information after obtaining your consent. Unless it is required by the relevant laws and regulations for you to provide such information, refusing to provide such information will only result in you're not being able to use the specific functions relating to such information, but does not affect your normal use of other functions of Min Wang.
  • At present, Min Wang will not actively share or transfer your device information to any third parties. Where the sharing or transferring of your device information arises in some other situations, we will obtain your consent.
  • At present, Min Wang will not actively obtain your device information from a third party. If there is a requirement to obtain your device information indirectly from a third party for future business development purposes, we will clarify with you the source, type and scope of such device information. For example, if the specific development is beyond the scope of what you originally agreed to provide to a third party, we will obtain your prior express consent before processing your device information, and we will strictly abide by the relevant laws and regulations and require that the third party guarantees the legitimacy of the information provided.

In order to help you better use our products or services, we may collect and use your relevant information.
